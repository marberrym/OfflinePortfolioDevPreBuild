About the Developer

My name is Matthew Marberry and I'm a driven software engineer committed to providing reliable solutions for real-world problems. I value exhibiting craftsmanship, passion, integrity and creative problem-solving in everything that I do. I strive to share my experience, strength and knowledge to benefit those around me. I live a civic oriented life, and the concept of, 'Paying it forward' is something that is very important to me.

I received my foundation working in the hospitality management industry, until I chose to follow my dreams and become an aspiring software engineer. The hospitality industry instilled me with a strong set of communication skills. I excel at team-building, collaboration, conflict resolution, and customer relation management. These are all skills I will carry with me as I grow as a software engineer.

Software development has always been a passion of mine over the years. I recently decided to truly kick start my career as a software engineer and I aimed to hone and develop my coding skills at Digital Crafts in Atlanta. Throughout the course at DigitalCrafts I have grown exponentially and am able to build reliable solutions for real-world problems.

My dedication and perseverance as I have grown as a developer have truly paid off. I know that I have a valuable skillset to bring with me wherever my future may lead me. I look forward to using my code to make a difference in the world, both inside and outside of the workplace.
