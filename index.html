When the idea of Hidden Gem Atlanta came into being, we wanted to create an app that would both provide each user with a valuable experience, but also helped the local community thrive. While there are many rating sites that thrive off of user generated content, we could not think of one that focused on regionally unique locations. We wanted to bridge that gap.

Hidden Gem Atlanta is somewhere that a user can go to share their experiences - or view the experiences of others.
